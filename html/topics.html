Magnifica Humanitas

Read by topic — pick what speaks to you

  1. Topic One Jobs and Automation

    Machines are learning to do more and more of the work people used to do. That can free us from boring tasks, but it can also leave families without a paycheck. Work is not just about money — it gives meaning, dignity, and a place in the community.

    Key takeaway: A good economy uses AI to help workers, not replace them.

  2. Topic Two AI and Machines vs. the Human Soul

    AI can write, draw, and even sound like it feels. But it does not have a soul, a conscience, or a heart that loves. Mixing up a clever machine with a real person is a mistake — we are not just data and outputs.

    Key takeaway: No matter how smart AI gets, only humans have a soul.

  3. Topic Three The Risks of Big Tech

    A handful of giant companies hold most of the data, the models, and the money. That gives them huge power over what we see, buy, and believe. Without rules and balance, this power can crush small voices and shape politics from the shadows.

    Key takeaway: When too few hands hold too much power, everyone else pays the price.

  4. Topic Four Tips on How to Live with AI

    AI is here to stay, so the question is how to live with it well. Use it as a tool, not a master. Double-check what it tells you. Protect time for real people, silence, and prayer. Teach kids to think before they click.

    Key takeaway: Stay the driver — let AI be the car, never the road.
