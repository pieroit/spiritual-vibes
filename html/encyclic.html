Magnifica Humanitas

Encyclical letter of Pope Leo XIV — 15 May 2026

  1. Chapter One A Dynamic Approach Faithful to the Gospel

    For over a century the Church has been writing a kind of "user manual" for living together well, updated pope after pope. AI shows up now as a huge new question — so big that it forces this manual to grow a new chapter.

    Key takeaway: AI is not just a tech story. It pushes the Church to rethink how we live together.

  2. Chapter Two Foundations and Principles of the Social Doctrine of the Church

    A short toolkit of big ideas: every person matters, the planet's goods are for everyone, decisions belong as close to people as possible, we are all in this together, and fairness is non-negotiable. These are the lenses to look at AI through.

    Key takeaway: Before asking "what can AI do?", ask "does it respect people and the common good?"

  3. Chapter Three Technology and Dominance — The Grandeur of Humanity in Light of the Promises of AI

    When we believe technology can fix everything, we start treating people like machines. AI promises amazing things, but it can also flatten what makes us human: our soul, our limits, our relationships. The chapter reminds us we are more than data.

    Key takeaway: A smarter machine is not the same as a greater human. Don't confuse the two.

  4. Chapter Four Safeguarding Humanity at a Time of Transformation — Truth, Work, Freedom

    AI is already changing three things we depend on every day: what we believe is true (fakes and deepfakes), how we earn a living (jobs and automation), and how freely we choose (algorithms that nudge us). Each one needs protection.

    Key takeaway: Guard your truth, your work, and your freedom — AI will test all three.

  5. Chapter Five The Culture of Power and the Civilization of Love

    Two roads open up. One uses AI to dominate — drones, weapons, countries pulling apart. The other uses it to serve — talking, healing, building peace together. The Pope asks: which one are we paving?

    Key takeaway: Powerful tools amplify our choices. Choose love over force.
